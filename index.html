Our Story

Crafted with Passion,
Poured with Purpose

Copper & Grain was born in a small garage in Denver's Highland neighborhood, where founder Thomas Wren began experimenting with wild yeast strains and locally sourced grain. His obsession? Creating beers that feel like a warm conversation — unpretentious, honest, and worth returning to.

"Great beer isn't just brewed — it's listened to. We follow the grain, the season, and the moment."

Nearly four decades later, our brewery stands on the same values that started it: local ingredients, slow fermentation, and the belief that a truly great pint should never be rushed. Every barrel we produce is a tribute to craftsmanship, community, and the simple joy of sharing something remarkable.

We partner with Colorado farmers, champion sustainable brewing practices, and invest in the neighborhoods that inspired us. Because great beer, like great community, is built one relationship at a time.
